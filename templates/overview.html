Project Overview Impacts of COVID-19 among inIndia,Brazil,Egypt and Egypt
Covid-19 is now a growing threat to the world.

Therefore, it is necessary to provide the public and decision-makers
with the means to visually visualize the epidemic data.

This project mainly provides data visualization for users from the perspectives of
static display and interaction, so that users can intuitively
understand epidemic data from different aspects.

The site contains four major tags:

1.epidemic trends,
2.strategy presentation,
3.impact and response,
4. multi-role collaboration, and impact communities.
