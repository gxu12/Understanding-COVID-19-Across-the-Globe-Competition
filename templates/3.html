The unemployment rate

In today's outbreak, the first casualty is people's income,

important measure of people's income. As you can see from the chart below, we looked at the employment rates of people in four different countries from October 2019 to July 2020. We can clearly see that in the beginning,

Brazil and Indonesia had the highest unemployment rates, at 11.6 and 11.3, compared with India and Egypt, at 8.1 and 6.8.

With the outbreak of the epidemic, the unemployment rate in India increased sharply after March 2020 and peaked at 23.5 in April 2020, while Brazil, Indonesia and Egypt showed a trend of gradual increase.
